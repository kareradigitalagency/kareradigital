FAQ

Common questions

Things people usually want to know before they start.

How long does a website take to build?
A standard 5-page business website is delivered in 5–7 business days after we receive your content and feedback. E-commerce or custom websites take 2–3 weeks. We always give you a realistic timeline before we start.
What is the minimum contract length?
Monthly plans require a minimum 3-month commitment — digital marketing takes time to show results. After that, you can continue month-to-month or pause anytime with 30 days notice.
How do I pay? Do you take UPI?
Yes — UPI, PhonePe, Google Pay, and bank transfer are all accepted. Monthly plans are paid in advance. For one-time projects, we take 50% to start and 50% on delivery.
Do you work with businesses outside Karera?
Absolutely. We work with businesses across Madhya Pradesh and India. Everything is handled online — strategy calls, reviews, and updates happen over WhatsApp and video call.
Do you create the social media content or do I?
We handle everything — design, copywriting, captions, and hashtags. You provide product photos or basic information about what to promote. We do the creative work from there.
What results can I realistically expect?
We won't promise specific numbers — results depend on your industry, budget, and market. What we do promise is consistent, quality work and transparent monthly reporting so you always know exactly what's happening.
